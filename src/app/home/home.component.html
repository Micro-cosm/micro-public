Q&A About WeJaFoo

What is an Access Provider?

An Access Provider is any potential point of access for businesses to engage underserved populations and/or support services. Access Providers can be:

  • Traditional disability service providers or support organizations;
  • Public, charitable, or privately funded community organizations;
  • Educational institutions, military institutions; and
  • Disability advocacy and support organizations.

What is WeJaFoo?

WeJaFoo is a cloud-based service built to position you for success in partnering with companies that genuinely value the role you play and the people you serve. WeJaFoo is a free marketplace for Access Providers. We’re here to help mainstream your services to a broad universe of employers and the thousands of jobs they need to fill. You need a pool of employers willing to hire your people and leverage your support services to make inclusion successful. Your job seekers stay yours. Employers need an efficient way to connect to you, relative to the business problems they need to solve. WeJaFoo provides all this and more.

What isn't WeJaFoo?

WeJaFoo is not business as usual. It’s not more work for the same results. WeJaFoo is not a job board for Diversity and Inclusion or a simple matching service with employers offering a few token jobs in your geography to meet compliance. It’s not a one-off tool for employers to hire opportunistically. It is not a charity. It’s about finding jobs for people, but it’s also bigger than that. It’s about true economic inclusion. Together with WeJaStrat, WeJaFoo is reshaping how business approaches this space and gives you a starring role.

How is WeJaFoo different?

What if employers came to you seeking your talent and services on a large scale? What if you were able to play an active role in supporting your consumers and job seekers from the start of the hiring process and not simply leave their success to chance? WeJaFoo provides an opportunity for a different conversation with business leaders. We help you speak directly to the right people about your organizational strengths and the job-seekers you represent. We are not simply creating awareness. We are driving action.

By being part of the world’s largest Access Provider network, you’ll literally be changing the world, enabling the success of your organization and the people you serve. Your consumers stay yours.
